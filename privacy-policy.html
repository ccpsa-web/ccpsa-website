Privacy Policy

Protecting your privacy is important to us

Section A: Website Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

When you visit our website, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Browsing data and device information
  • Information from form submissions (contact requests, inquiries)
  • Analytics data from essential cookies only

How We Use Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Improve website functionality and user experience
  • Respond to your inquiries and requests
  • Understand user behavior and website performance
  • Comply with applicable regulations and legal obligations

Third-Party Services

Our website may integrate with third-party services:

  • Patient Portal: Epic MyChart (secure healthcare portal)
  • Online Scheduling: Integrated appointment system
  • Analytics: Anonymized data to improve site performance

These services have their own privacy policies governing data collection and use.

Data Security

We use SSL/TLS encryption to protect information transmitted through our website. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Contact for Privacy Questions

For privacy-related questions about this website, contact us:

Section B: Notice of Privacy Practices (HIPAA)

How We Use Your Medical Information

CCPSA uses your protected health information (PHI) for the following purposes:

  • Treatment: Providing and coordinating patient care
  • Payment: Processing insurance claims and billing
  • Healthcare Operations: Managing practice operations and quality improvement

Other Uses of Your Medical Information

We may use and disclose your medical information without authorization for:

  • Public health activities and disease reporting
  • Law enforcement purposes (subpoenas, court orders)
  • Prevention of serious threats to health or safety
  • Workers' compensation claims
  • Medical research (with appropriate safeguards)
  • Communication with family members or emergency contacts

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your medical records
  • Request amendment or correction of medical information
  • Receive an accounting of disclosures of your medical information
  • Request restrictions on use and disclosure of medical information
  • Request confidential communications of your medical information
  • Receive a copy of this Notice of Privacy Practices
  • Receive notification if there is a breach of your unsecured medical information

Filing a Complaint
