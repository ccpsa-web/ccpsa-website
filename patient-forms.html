Before Your Appointment

  • Arrive 15 minutes early if paperwork is complete, 30 minutes early if not
  • Bring CDs of all relevant chest imaging (X-ray, CT, PET CT)
  • Bring information from recent doctor visits or hospitalizations
  • Bring an up-to-date medication list
